About TEDxChennai Institute of Technology

Questions for the Future

Our theme, “Questions for the Future?”, reflects our belief that the best ideas often start with a question. As we look ahead, the unknowns seem more complex, urgent, and interconnected than ever. This theme reflects this spirit, as our world faces transformative challenges in technology, climate, social issues, and more.

By asking the right questions, we open doors to endless possibilities and inspire innovative solutions; we’re embracing curiosity and the courage to explore the unknown, building a bridge to whatever comes next. Together, let’s answer the questions that will shape our tomorrow.
